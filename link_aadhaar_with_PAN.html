Link Aadhaar with PAN (Online & SMS)

by Sanjeev

Post Updated on May 18, 2018 at 04:25 PM

The government of India seems to be encouraging the use of Aadhaar card in the majority of public services or wherever Aadhaar can be used. In the past couple of years, we have seen the use of Aadhaar has increased multiple times; from banking to the telecom industry, wherever there is need of collecting users details, Aadhar comes in. The main purpose of it is to verify the identity of the user, it helps in stopping frauds and other illegal activities.

Income tax department has stated that income tax returns couldn't be filed online without linking Aadhaar with PAN. The income tax department also has released the deadline for linking the two, which was 31 March 2018. However, later, the deadline was extended till 30 June 2018.

Before starting this process, make sure that your details like Name, DOB and gender are same in your Aadhaar and PAN. Even the slightest mismatch in the details can be the reason for rejection of linking the two.

There are mainly two ways you can use to link the two identities. We will go through both of them.

Link Aadhaar to PAN Through SMS (Easy and Recommended)

This method is both easy and recommended. You can use any mobile number to send SMS. The income tax department has issued two mobile numbers (567678 or 56161), where you can send SMS in a below-given format.

UIDPAN (Space) <12 digit Aadhaar Number> (Space) <10 digit PAN Number>

E.g. UIDPAN 123456789012 AAAAA9999A

Send it in this format to either 567678 or 56161.

Link Aadhaar to PAN Through Website

If you ever went to the income tax e-Filing website (https://www.incometaxindiaefiling.gov.in/home), and if you have registered or created an account there using your PAN details, you can simply log in with your details.

First Method (You need to be registered on Income Tax E-Filling Website)

After visiting the income tax e-Filling website. You may see the login button there, just click on it. It will transfer you to the login page.

Put your login details and click on login.

A popup will appear asking you to link your Aadhaar with PAN. If it doesn't appear, simply click on the Profile Settings > Link Aadhaar. You will land on the below page.

You will see few details already filled, like your Name, DOB and gender. You just need to fill your Aadhaar number. Enter your Aadhar number and fill the Captcha(it is to verify that you're not a bot/computer program).

Done! A pop-up will appear with a message; Aadhaar-PAN linking is completed successfully.  

Second Method (You don't need to be registered on Income Tax E-Filling Website)

Visit (https://www.incometaxindiaefiling.gov.in/home). Under the Quick Links sidebar, you will see Link Aadhaar (click on it).

Here, you will be asked to enter some required details. Like PAN number, Aadhaar Number, Name. Tick on I agree to validate... and then fill the Captcha. Now click on Link Aadhaar button.

Done!

Conclusion

Usually, the linking is done instantly. However, if in your case it gets delayed for hours or even days, you can always retry. If still doesn't work then contact to Income tax department through here: (https://www.incometaxindiaefiling.gov.in/main/contactUs)

Once linked, you can confirm the linking by logging the website and click on Profile Settings > Link Aadhaar.
